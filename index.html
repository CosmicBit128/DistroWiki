Distro Wiki

I've always hated how disconnected all linux distros are. If you are a new user, you find yourself watching 5 different videos explaining so much details about every distro with unncecessary details. That's why I made this website. It's a guide for newcomers to find a distro they like with a small and simple description.

What is a distro?

Think of a distro as a "version" of linux. It's a set of pre-set packages and settings that you can modify as much as you want.
